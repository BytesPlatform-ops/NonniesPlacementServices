Part 2: Multistep Client Intake Flow

Please refactor our public onboarding form into a multi-step wizard component featuring persistent progress bars, conditional logical routing, and simple form controls to minimize typing for stressed families.

Step 1: The Basics (Who & Where)

* Who is filling out this form?
* UI Control: Radio Buttons
   * Options: Family Member / Legal Guardian | Hospital Discharge Planner | Case Manager / Social Worker
* Where is the individual currently located?
* UI Control: Radio Buttons
   * Options: At Home (Safe) | At Home (In Crisis) | Hospital ER / Med-Surg Unit | Psychiatric Facility | Rehab / Skilled Nursing Facility
* Target Move-In Timeline:
* UI Control: Radio Buttons
   * Options: Immediate (Hospital Discharge / Emergency) | Within 1 to 2 Weeks | Within 30 Days | Planning for the Future
* Conditional Logic Rule: If "Hospital ER" or "Immediate" options are toggled positive, flag this submission with a high-priority tag (status: high_urgency) on our backend administrative interface.

Step 2: Cognitive & Memory Support Needs (Dementia/Alzheimer's)

* Does the individual have a diagnosed cognitive condition? (Select all that apply)
* UI Control: Checkboxes
   * Options: Alzheimer's Disease | Dementia (Lewy Body, Vascular, Frontotemporal, etc.) | Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) | No formal diagnosis, but experiencing severe short-term memory loss
* Safety & Cognitive Behaviors (Select all that apply):
* UI Control: Checkboxes
   * Options:
   * [ ] Exit-Seeking / Wandering: Attempts to leave the home environment or gets lost easily.
      * [ ] Sundowning: Experiences increased confusion, anxiety, or agitation in the late afternoon/evening.
      * [ ] Orientation: Disoriented to time, place, or recognizing familiar family members.
      * [ ] Requires a fully secured, locked memory care perimeter or community structure.
   
Step 3: Mental & Behavioral Health Profiles

* Are there active mental health diagnoses requiring specialized tracking? (Select all that apply)
* UI Control: Checkboxes
   * Options: Chronic Depression / Anxiety | Bipolar Disorder | Schizophrenia / Schizoaffective Disorder | PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder) | History of Substance Use / Addiction Support Needs
* Current Behavioral Care Expressions (Select all that apply):
* UI Control: Checkboxes
   * Options:
   * [ ] Verbal Aggression: Frequent shouting, resistance to care routines, or repetitive vocalizations.
      * [ ] Physical Aggression: Pushing, scratching, or combative behavior during hands-on care (bathing/dressing).
      * [ ] Hallucinations / Delusions: Sees or hears things that aren't there, or holds fixed false beliefs.
      * [ ] Hoarding / Shadows: Collects items obsessively or exhibits intense pacing.
   
Step 4: Medical & Physical Level of Care

* Mobility Support Level:
* UI Control: Radio Buttons
   * Options: Walks independently | Uses walker/wheelchair independently | Requires 1-person physical assistance to stand/transfer | Requires 2-person assistance or a mechanical lift (Hoyer Lift) | Bedbound
* Specialized Medical Interventions Required (Select all that apply):
* UI Control: Checkboxes
   * Options: Insulin-Dependent Diabetes Management (Sliding scale / RN injection) | Continuous Oxygen Therapy or CPAP | Wound Care (Stage 2, 3, or 4 pressure ulcers) | Catheter / Ostomy care management | Hospice Care or Palliative Comfort Care services
* Medication Assistance:
* UI Control: Radio Buttons
   * Options: Manages own medications | Needs reminders | Requires full administration, crushing, or locked storage due to refusal or safety risks

Step 5: Financial Capabilities & Submission

* How will care be financed? (Select all that apply)
* UI Control: Checkboxes
   * Options: Private Pay (Savings, home sale, assets) | WA State Medicaid (Long-Term Care / COPES program active) | Medicaid Pending (Financial spend-down currently in progress) | VA Benefits (Aid & Attendance) | Long-Term Care Insurance Policy
* Estimated Monthly Care Budget:
* UI Control: Radio Buttons
   * Options: Under $4,000 / mo | $4,000 to $6,000 / mo | $6,000 to $8,000 / mo | $8,000+ / mo
* Form Action Submit Button: Text label must read exactly: [ Lock In Profile & Connect with an RN ]
